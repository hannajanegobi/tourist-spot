WELCOME TO MAHAGNAO TOURIST SPOT

Discover the natural wonders and breathtaking beauty of Mahagnao. Explore the Mahagnao Volcano National Park, enjoy the pristine Malagsum Lake, and immerse yourself in the rich culture and warm hospitality of the local community.

ATTRACTIONS

MAHAGNAO VOLCANO NATIONAL PARK

Located in Burauen, Leyte, the Mahagnao Volcano National Park is a must-visit destination. It offers breathtaking views of craters, hot springs, multi-colored mud, virgin forests, and a beautiful lagoon. Don't miss the opportunity to explore the two volcanic lakes, Mahagnao Lake and Malagsum Lake, which are surrounded by lush greenery.

MALAGSUM LAKE

Malagsum Lake is a stunning crater lake within the Mahagnao Volcano National Park. Its clear waters are perfect for swimming and provide a refreshing escape from the heat. Enjoy a peaceful boat ride and immerse yourself in the beauty of this natural wonder.
